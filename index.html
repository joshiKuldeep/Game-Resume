Eduzo Dashboard

The Eduzo Dashboard is an innovative platform designed to transform education by offering a more interactive, personalized learning experience. Tailored specifically for schools, the dashboard allows administrators to create classes, add students, and give them access to a variety of engaging educational games. These games, built on Motion Tracking, Computer Vision, Augmented Reality, Flash Card, and Artificial Intelligence technologies, provide both Teach Mode and Test Mode to foster understanding and evaluate performance.

One of the standout features of the platform is the generation of automated AI-powered reports. In addition to the traditional marksheets, these AI reports give a detailed analysis of each student's performance, providing insights into their learning patterns, strengths, and areas for improvement.

The dashboard utilizes motion tracking and AR to deliver immersive learning experiences, making education fun and engaging. After playing the games, students not only receive their scores but also benefit from the AI reports that guide their learning journey. These tools are designed with insights from researchers at Stanford University, helping children learn at their own pace in a stress-free environment that encourages making mistakes and learning from them.
